# 🚀 Neon Defender: Tower Defense

A fast-paced, neon-styled Tower Defense game built with HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript. Designed to be lightweight, single-file, and playable directly from a browser via GitHub Pages.

## 🎮 How to Play
1. **Select a Tower**: Use the selection bar at the bottom to choose one of the 6 tower types.
2. **Build**: Click anywhere on the dark area to place your selected tower.
3. **Upgrade**: Click on an **existing tower** to upgrade its stats (Damage, Range, or Fire Rate). Upgrading costs money!
4. **Defend**: Don't let the enemies reach the end of the path.

## 🏰 Tower Types
| Tower | Color | Special Ability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic** | Blue | Reliable all-rounder. |
| **Sniper** | Green | High damage, very long range, slow fire rate. |
| **Rapid** | Red | Very fast firing, but low damage per shot. |
| **Splash** | Yellow | Deals area-of-effect (AOE) damage to nearby enemies. |
| **Frost** | Purple | Slows enemies down significantly. |
| **Tesla** | Cyan | High damage, high-tech electric pulses. |

## 👾 Enemies
* **Scout (Small/Fast):** High speed, low health.
* **Grunt (Medium):** The standard enemy.
* **Tank (Large/Slow):** Massive health, very slow.

## 💰 Pro-Tip
Reach **Wave 30** to receive a massive **$500 Gold Bonus** to help you upgrade your defenses for the endgame!

## 🛠️ Setup for GitHub Pages
1. Upload `index.html` to your repository.
2. Go to **Settings > Pages**.
3. Select the `main` branch and click **Save**.
4. Your game will be live at `https://<your-username>.github.io/<your-repo-name>/`
